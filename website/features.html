Lead management

Never lose a lead to a missed follow-up again

Every enquiry from every channel — WhatsApp, web form, ads, manual entry — lands in one pipeline. AI scores it instantly. The right rep is assigned automatically. The follow-up never slips.

  • Capture leads from WhatsApp, web forms, ads, and manual entry
  • AI lead scoring — Hot, Warm, Cold in seconds
  • Pipeline stages — New → Contacted → Demo → Converted
