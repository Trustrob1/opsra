Common questions

Everything you need to know

Is Opsra only for businesses that sell on WhatsApp?
No. Opsra works for SaaS companies, real estate firms, healthcare providers, insurance companies, schools, and any business that manages leads, customers, teams, or contractors. The customer communication module supports WhatsApp, email, and broadcast messaging — you use what fits your business.
Can I start with one module and add more later?
Yes. Every plan is modular. Start with what your business needs most urgently and expand as your team grows. We will recommend where to start based on your biggest pain point.
What does the setup fee cover?
Full configuration of your organisation, onboarding your team, importing existing data, and a structured training session. One-time cost from ₦75,000 depending on complexity. This is how we make sure Opsra is actually working for your business from day one — not just installed.
What if my team doesn't adopt it?
Adoption is the most common concern and it is why the setup session exists. We configure Opsra around how your team already works, not the other way around. Most teams are logging daily and using the pipeline within the first two weeks.
Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?
Yes. You can start monthly and move to annual at any point to unlock the 2-month saving. Annual billing is invoiced upfront and saves you the equivalent of two months per year.
